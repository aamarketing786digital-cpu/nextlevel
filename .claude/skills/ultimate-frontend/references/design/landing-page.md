## Core structure
1. **Above the fold**: Headline (Outcome), Subheadline (Clarity), Primary CTA (Verb).
2. **Social Proof**: Logos/Stats immediately visible.
3. **Problem/Solution**: Connect pain to product.
4. **Benefits**: 3-5 outcome-driven points.
5. **How it works**: 3 simple steps.
6. **FAQ**: Address objections.
7. **Final CTA**: Recap offer.

## Copywriting tips
- **Specific**: "Save 4 hours/week" > "Save time"
- **Risk Reversal**: "No credit card required" / "Cancel anytime"
- **Clear CTA**: "Start Free Trial" > "Submit"
